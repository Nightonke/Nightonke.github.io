Open-Source

Some open-source libraries from college.

BMB iOS

BMB(Boom Menu Button) iOS是一款可以从悬浮按钮、菜单按钮爆炸展开的菜单开源库。提供4种图文样式，同时兼顾高度定制、接口易使用的特性。

BMB(Boom Menu Button) iOS is a menu which can boom from a floating action button or menu button in action bar. 4 kinds of sub buttons with texts or images for high customization and easy coding. Ease, boom and other animations make menu different and creative.

BMB iOS

BMB Android

BMB(Boom Menu Button) Android是一款可以从悬浮按钮、菜单按钮爆炸展开的菜单开源库。提供4种图文样式，同时兼顾高度定制、接口易使用的特性。

BMB(Boom Menu Button) Android is a menu which can boom from a floating action button or menu button in action bar. 4 kinds of sub buttons with texts or images for high customization and easy coding. Ease, boom and other animations make menu different and creative.

BMB Android

WoWoViewPager

WoWoViewPager可以优化App引导页面、制作简历App。它将动画和ViewPager结合在了一起，当你在滑动ViewPager时，你也在控制帧动画，恍若时光往复。

WoWoViewPager optimizes scrolling app intros or making your CV app. It combines animations and viewpager. When you are swiping viewpager, you are also controlling the frames of the animation. Just like rewinding time. You are the director of the animations.

WoWoViewPager

BlurLockView

一款可高度定制的高斯模糊解锁页面。

A lock view with blur effect, which is easy to customize.

BlurLockView

JellyToggleButton

JellyToggleButton是一款果冻状的开关按钮，提供18*30种“果冻口味”，当然你也可以定制自己的独特口味。

JellyToggleButton is a cute toggle button with 18 jelly types and 30 ease types. You can also define your own style and have your JTB custom-made.

JellyToggleButton

FaceOffToggleButton

一款“开则开心，关则自闭”的开关按钮。

Toggle button which shows a happy face for checked or unhappy for unchecked.

FaceOffToggleButton

Applications / Projects

Applications or projects developed by me or my team.

QQMusic

QQ音乐是隶属于腾讯音乐娱乐集团的音乐流媒体平台。自2005年创立至今，QQ音乐注册用户总量已达8亿。我在其中负责iOS客户端开发。

QQ Music is a Chinese music streaming and download service owned by Tencent. It has more than 800 million users, 400 million monthly active users, 100 million daily active users and 10 million subscribers. It has a 15% share of the Chinese online music market. I work as an iOS engineer of QQ Music Team in Tencent.

QQMusic

Braeco

Braeco(杯口)是我和其他朋友在大学时期的创业项目，是一款餐饮管理系统。用户通过扫码点餐，餐厅可以在手机和电脑上接收订单、收款、打印小票、分析营收情况。 广州大学城的许多餐厅都使用了Braeco。杯口包含H5、Android、iOS、PC以及小票机系统，我在其中独立负责Android手机和Pad的开发工作。

Braeco is a management platform for catering enterprises. It contains iOS, android, PC, web and other devices like small ticket machine, printer and so on. This system is widely used in Guangzhou Higher Education Mega Center, China. I develop android(phone and pad) for braeco.

Braeco

CoCoin

CoCoin是我大二～大三独立维护的项目，包括产品、设计、iOS端、后台的工作。它是一款提供多分析视图的账本App，可方便地记录、云同步个人帐户，同时帮助用户全面细致地分析收支情况。

Multi-view accounting application, which helps keep personal accounts conveniently, upload and sync accounts and check expanses comprehensively.

CoCoin

Gitee

Gitee是一款OSX的状态栏应用，通过定时拉取Github的API，可以展示Github上的状态、事件、热点等，还能帮助分析stars、followers的变化趋势。

Gitee is a OSX status bar application updating data periodically, like contributions, stargazers and followers statistics, trendings, notifications and so on.

Gitee
