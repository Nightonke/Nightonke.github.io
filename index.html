Open-Source

BMB iOS

BMB(Boom Menu Button) iOS是一款可以从悬浮按钮、菜单按钮爆炸展开的菜单开源库。提供4种图文样式，同时兼顾高度定制、接口易使用的特性。

BMB(Boom Menu Button) iOS is a menu which can boom from a floating action button or menu button in action bar. 4 kinds of sub buttons with texts or images for high customization and easy coding. Ease, boom and other animations make menu different and creative.

BMB iOS

WoWoViewPager

WoWoViewPager可以优化App引导页面、制作简历App。它将动画和ViewPager结合在了一起，当你在滑动ViewPager时，你也在控制帧动画，恍若时光往复。

WoWoViewPager optimizes scrolling app intros or making your CV app. It combines animations and viewpager. When you are swiping viewpager, you are also controlling the frames of the animation. Just like rewinding time. You are the director of the animations.

WoWoViewPager

JellyToggleButton

JellyToggleButton是一款果冻状的开关按钮，提供18*30种“果冻口味”，当然你也可以定制自己的独特口味。

JellyToggleButton is a cute toggle button with 18 jelly types and 30 ease types. You can also define your own style and have your JTB custom-made.

JellyToggleButton
